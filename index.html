About Our Hostel


B.K.Mariappa’s Hostel was started on 01-07-1921 with free lodging and boarding facilities entirely on secular basis for deserving poor students. So far thousands of students have been benefited from this institution and all of them are well settled in life serving the society all-round the country and abroad.


B.K.Mariappa’s Hostel Old Boys’ Association was formed in 1946 and it helped the Charities to get more income by way of rents from the buildings, constructed out of the donations from the old boys and philanthropists which facilitated to accommodate more number of boy students.


The Girls’ Hostel started during 1999 in a rented building was inaugurated by Sri H.Narasimhaiah, an old student of our Hostel, follower of Gandhian philosophy and former Vice Chancellor of Bengaluru University. During 2002 it was moved to our own building, inaugurated by Dr. Kiran Bedi I.P.S. and this accommodated more number of girl students.


The Students’ Association, formed under the style, Ashoka Sangha during 1947 just after procuring independence with a view to inculcate the noble teachings of Emperor Ashoka is enriching the students’ literary and sports activities. Many Inter Hostel competitions are conducted every year, prizes are awarded and Rolling Shields presented.
